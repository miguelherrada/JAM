JAM: The Jacobian Analytical Method

JAM is a powerful numerical method for solving complex non-linear problems, particularly in fluid dynamics with free interfaces. It leverages analytical Jacobians to achieve high efficiency and accuracy across implementations in Python, Matlab, and Julia.
